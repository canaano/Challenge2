About Canaan

My name is Canaan Owens. I was born and raised in Huntington Beach, Californa. I have flown drones professionally for the last 5 years, mostly in the surfing industry. Although, I will never stop flying, I felt it was time to transition into a more long term career. In September of 2022, I made the first step toward a future in software development. I hope that this slight pivot on my professional path with allow me to not only continue with my passion of drone videography but also, give me the tools needed to succed in Programming.
